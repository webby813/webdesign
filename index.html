About New Era University Australian Mathematics Competition



The Australian Mathematics Competition (AMC) is a prestigious and widely recognized event that brings together students from across the globe to engage in challenging mathematical problems. This esteemed competition, which has garnered international acclaim, serves as a platform for young mathematicians to test their skills and gain valuable experience in a highly competitive environment. By doing so, the university provides its students with the opportunity to showcase their exceptional mathematical prowess on an international stage, enabling them to gain recognition and build confidence in their abilities. The participation in AMC not only enhances the students' problem-solving skills but also promotes a spirit of camaraderie and academic enthusiasm within the university community.
