Introduction

About New Era University Australian Mathematics Competition



The Australian Mathematics Competition (AMC) is a prestigious, globally recognized event that gathers students from around the world to tackle challenging mathematical problems. Esteemed for its high standards and international acclaim, the AMC provides a platform for young mathematicians to test their skills and gain valuable experience in a competitive setting. Through participation, students have the opportunity to demonstrate their exceptional mathematical abilities on an international stage, gaining recognition and boosting their confidence. Involvement in the AMC not only sharpens students' problem-solving skills but also fosters a sense of camaraderie and academic enthusiasm within the university community.

Our Objectives

Achieve
