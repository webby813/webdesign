Award and Certification

Awards and Certificates in the Australian Mathematics Competition


The Australian Mathematics Competition (AMC) is renowned not only for its challenging problems but also for the prestigious awards and certificates it offers to participants. This competition, celebrated for its rigorous standards and high level of difficulty, attracts mathematically talented students from all over the world. Students from New Era University who take part in the AMC have the opportunity to earn various levels of recognition, reflecting their performance, dedication, and exceptional aptitude in mathematics. These awards and certificates serve as a testament to their hard work and intellectual achievements, offering them not only personal satisfaction but also a valuable addition to their academic portfolios. Participation in the AMC helps to foster a deeper appreciation for mathematics, encourages a pursuit of excellence, and inspires students to reach new heights in their academic endeavors.



Certificates of Participation and Credit


Every student who participates in the AMC receives a Certificate of Participation. This certificate acknowledges their effort and enthusiasm for tackling complex mathematical problems, serving as a valuable addition to their academic portfolio. The Certificate of Participation from the AMC is crafted with high-quality paper, featuring an elegant design that includes the AMC logo, the participant's name, and the year of participation. It serves as a tangible reminder of their involvement in this international event
