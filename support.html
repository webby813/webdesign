Help & Feedback

Participation Overview

The Australian Mathematics Competition (AMC) provides a platform for students to challenge their mathematical abilities and gain recognition for their skills. Your participation in the AMC is commendable, and we hope this feedback will help you improve and excel further.

Checking Results

You can view your AMC results on the official AMC website. Simply visit the Results page and enter your participant details. For any queries or support, contact the AMC helpline at [AMC Helpline Number].

Preparation and Resources

To prepare effectively for future competitions, explore the AMC Preparation Guide on our website. This guide includes practice problems, study tips, and detailed explanations to help you strengthen your mathematical skills.

Find Training Centres

Locate AMC training centers using our Training Centre Locator to find support near you.

Educational Discounts

Students and teachers can avail of special discounts on study materials and resources. Visit our Education Store or call [AMC Education Support Number].

Authorised Resellers

Find AMC authorized resellers for additional resources and support. Use our Reseller Locator to find a reseller in your area.

By following this guidance and utilizing the available resources, you can enhance your skills and perform even better in future AMC competitions. Keep practicing, stay motivated, and continue to challenge yourself.
